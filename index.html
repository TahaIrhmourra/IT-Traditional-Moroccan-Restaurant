OUR STORY

Born from Passion, Established in early 2023, I&T© was the brainchild of IRHMOURRA TAHA. Driven by a passion for sharing the vibrant flavors of Moroccan cuisine with the world, I&T© brings generations-old family recipes to life. Each dish is a delectable testament to tradition, crafted with love and the freshest ingredients.

OUR MISSION AND WHAT WE BELIEVE IN

As IRHMOURRA TAHA's grandfather used to say, "Don't fight your hunger, embrace it!" Here at I&T©, we believe that indulging in good food is a celebration of life's simple pleasures. We invite you to embark on a culinary journey through Morocco, where every bite is an explosion of flavor and a taste of heritage.
